LGBTQIA+

What is the LGBTQIA+ community?

The LGBTQIA+ community is a supportive community of people who identify differently than average people who are cisgender
and heterosexual.

What does "LGBTQIA" stand for?

"LGBTQIA" stands for Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, Transgender, Questioning, Intersex, and Asexual.
The plus that is sometimes added to the end represents all the identities that aren't included in the acronym, such as Pansexual, Demiromantic, Ally, etc...
