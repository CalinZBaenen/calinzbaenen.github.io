What is enby?

"Enby" is a word that comes from the sound of saying the letters 'N' and 'B', which stand for "nonbinary".
The word can refer to someone who is nonbinary, as in “They are an enby.”, or as in “The enby students.”.

Definition

Enby

  1. (noun) A person that is nonbinary.
  2. (adjective) That which has a gender identity that is neither (fully) male nor female, or is a combination of masculinity and femininity.
