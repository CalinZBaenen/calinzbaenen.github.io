What is nonbinary?

To be nonbinary is to have a gender-identity that is neither (fully) masculine nor feminine, or is a combination of masculinity and femininity.

Definition

Nonbinary

  1. (adjective) A gender identity which is neither (fully) masculine nor feminine, or is a combination of both masculinity and femininity.
