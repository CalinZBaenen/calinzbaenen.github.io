What is an adjective?

An adjective is a word that describes any trait of a nominal.
Adjectives are abstract and versatile, as they can describe anything, such as size, shape, color, rightness, etc...

Here are some examples of adjectives:

  • Helpful
  • Red
  • Large
  • Complicated
  • Sad
  • Intelligent
