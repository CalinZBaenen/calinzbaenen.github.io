Furries

What or who is a "furry"?

Furries are groups of people interested in anthropomorphic animals. They can range from really furry like a bunny to scaley like a lizard.
This group of people is called the furry fandom.

From Oxford English Dictionary : "an enthusiast for animal characters with human characteristics, in particular a person who dresses up in costume as such a character or uses one as an avatar online.".

Are furries connected to the LGTBQIA+ community?

While there is overlap between the furry fandom and the LGTBQIA+ they are not directly connected.
Someone can be a furry without being in the LGBTQIA+ community, and vice-versa. And sometimes people are in both groups, such as me, a pansexual, non-binary furry.
