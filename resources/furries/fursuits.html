What is a fursuit?

"Fursuit" is a combination of the word "fur", commonly short for "furry", or someone in the furry fandom, and "suit", thus a "fursuit" is an outfit for a furry.

A fursuit is a costume that makes you look like an anthropomorphic animal.
Usually, fursuits come in parts: the head, paws, a body, the tail, accessories, etc...

Does every furry have a fursuit?

Not every furry, whether unfortunate for them or not, has a fursuit.
The truth of the matter is that fursuits are expensive and can only be bought from specialized vendors, making it taxing in price and convenience.

Do people in fursuits act like that animal?

No; people in fursuits do not, always, act like the animal of the suit they are in.
However, of course, there are going to be people that will.
