General Information

What is a furry?

A furry is a person who is, or has an interest in, anthropomorphic animals, or, more commonly, is a person with a persona that is a furry, called a fursona.

Who is a furry?

You can't just tell who is and who isn't a furry; it is a personly thing.
Some YouTubers that are furries include: TheOdd1sOut and Chipflake. Furries are also outside of the online-space too, as Malcolm Ray demonstrates by being an actor and voiceover-artist!

Are furries connected to the LGBTQIA+ community?

While there is overlap between the furry fandom and the LGBTQIA+, they are not directly connected.
Someone can be a furry without being in the LGBTQIA+ community and vice-versa. And sometimes people are in both groups, such as me, a pansexual, non-binary furry.
