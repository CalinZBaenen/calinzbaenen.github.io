15-bit Colorpicker

What is this?

This small program was designed to let me easily pick out colors from the from the 15-bit color spectrum.
You can enter a 15-bit colorcode or input a color.
  (If the given color is out of the 15BCS it will automatically convert it to the nearest color.)

What is 15-bit color?

15-bit color is a colorsystem where red, green, and blue use five bits each.
For comparison, most, if not all, modern computers use 32-bit color, usually RGB (Red Green Blue*), where red, green, and blue use eight bits each with an additional alpha channel.
