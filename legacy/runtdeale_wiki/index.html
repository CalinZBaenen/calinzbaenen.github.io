CHARACTERS

Chloe

Chloe /Kl-O-E/, the protagonist, is a human that suffers from depression. It is not shown, but she is unconcious (during the duration of the game); she then has to fight her emotions, quite literally, to wake up into the real world and stay alive. She overcomes many challenges, in an attempt to help herself, if she isn't trying to kill herself. Chloe is a 15 year old girl that despises everyone and everything.

Father Drinbull

Father Drinbull, with the real name "Froghurt", hates the conscious, especially with what they've been saying, not things about them, nor their kind, but more about the diseases spreading through the world, he is constantly paranoid his children will die from them, even a common cold. Despite trying not to say he has multiple children, he does have more than one child, the youngest being Bulseiy, 2 of his other children died, and the last one moved into the conscious world.

Mother Drinbull

Mother Drinbull, with the real name "Bella", is the nicest monster Chloe will see, she always has a nice attitude, and hates people that turn down an opportunity to help people. She is always there to make new friends, and help others in need.

Bulseiy

Bulseiy /bulz-I/, is a hyperactive child that loves going on adventures. The costume he sports is one he made for a party, he calls it "Royal Robe", it sports a circle with two wings, and a flower at the bottom. Bulseiy has always been intrested in visitors from the conscious world.

Mysterious Foxgirl

This character is shy, but yet they still confidently lurk within Chloe's subcon. Personality-wise, they are the opposite of Bulseiy.

Terry

Terry loves video games, despite not being able to play them. Terry had small hands to begin but they got cut off by Father Drinbull, as he deemed them unnecessary. Terry though, instead watched people play video games, his dream was to be a gaming champion, though he's a pushover because he never does anything.
